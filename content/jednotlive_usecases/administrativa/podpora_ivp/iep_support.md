# AI-Powered IEP Support: A Guide for Teachers

## Goal
Streamline the administrative burden of Individual Education Plans (IEPs) by using AI to brainstorm pedagogical strategies, draft learning goals, and adapt curriculum materials for students with diverse needs. This guide helps teachers provide more personalized support while ensuring strict data privacy.

### 🛠️ Recommended AI Tools
* **[ChatGPT](../../../jednotlive_tools/chatboti/chatgpt/chatgpt.md) / Claude:** Excellent for generating creative differentiation strategies and drafting structured goals.
* **Goblin Tools (Magic School AI alternative):** Useful for breaking down complex tasks into smaller, manageable steps for students with executive dysfunction.

---

## 🔄 The Step-by-Step Workflow

`[Identify Needs] ➔ [Anonymize Context] ➔ [Brainstorm Strategies] ➔ [Draft Goals] ➔ [Review & Refine]`

### Step 1: Identify Student Needs
Focus on the specific challenge (e.g., reading comprehension, sensory processing, or time management) without using identifiable personal data.

### Step 2: Input Anonymized Context
Describe the student's profile generally: "A 5th-grade student with ADHD who struggles with multi-step instructions."

### Step 3: Brainstorm Strategies
Ask the AI for evidence-based classroom accommodations or specific teaching techniques tailored to that profile.

### Step 4: Draft SMART Goals
Use the AI to refine raw ideas into Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound goals.

---

## ✍️ Effective Prompting

### Prompt Template:
> "Act as a Special Education specialist. I have a student in [Grade Level] who [Specific Challenge]. Suggest 5 evidence-based classroom accommodations and draft 2 SMART learning goals for [Subject/Skill]."

### Example Prompt:
> "Act as a Special Education specialist. I have a student in 7th-grade math who has high math anxiety and struggles to start word problems. Suggest 5 classroom accommodations to help them feel more confident and draft 2 SMART goals for their progress in solving linear equations."

---

## ⚠️ The "Human-in-the-Loop" Review Checklist
* **Privacy Check:** **MANDATORY:** Have you removed all names, birthdates, and IDs? Ensure the description is generic enough that the student cannot be identified.
* **Professional Judgment:** Does the suggested strategy align with your school's resources and the student's known strengths?
* **Compliance:** Ensure all AI-generated suggestions are reviewed by the school’s special education coordinator to meet legal requirements.

---

## ✅ How to Know You Have Understood This Guide
By the end of this guide, you should be able to:
* Generate a list of targeted classroom accommodations for a specific learning profile.
* Use AI to turn a general learning objective into a specific SMART goal.
* Anonymize student information effectively before using any AI tool.
