# Project Summary: AIPREMNA.SK

**AIPREMNA.SK** is a comprehensive educational platform and roadmap designed specifically for Slovak teachers. Its primary mission is to bridge the gap between advanced Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies and everyday classroom practice. The project teaches educators to view AI not as a replacement, but as a powerful assistant that helps them reclaim valuable time.

## Key Areas and Topics

*   **Fundamentals & Ethics:** An introduction to the world of AI, effective prompting techniques, and rules for safe usage, with an emphasis on student data protection and critical fact-checking (preventing hallucinations).
*   **Administration & Communication:** Guides for lightning-fast drafting of professional and empathetic emails for parents, colleagues, or school management.
*   **Lesson Preparation:** Automating the creation of detailed lesson plans, lesson outlines, generating accompanying images, and translating foreign-language materials.
*   **Material Creation:** Rapid generation of worksheets, practice questions, flashcards, and clear "cheatsheets" for students.
*   **Assessment & Feedback:** Efficient creation of exams (including A/B variants), drafting of grading rubrics, assistance with grading papers, and formulating constructive feedback.
*   **Innovative Tools & Personalization:** Utilizing tools like NotebookLM, creating summary podcasts, and techniques for differentiating curriculum for students with diverse needs and proficiency levels.

## Project Structure

All content is organized into practical "playbooks" that include specific prompt templates and checklists that teachers can immediately apply to their daily workflows.
